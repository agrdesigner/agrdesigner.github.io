UX case study
04

Stable Work: a platform that connects small businesses and sales professionals

Brand, design audit, product design, component library

Home page

Timeline: 2019-2020

Stable brings small businesses and sales professionals together in order to help them grow and scale their sales. This is done through their SaaS app, which handles payments, scheduling, and much more in order to streamline the sales process.

I worked alongside my design buddy, Gonzalo, bringing the sales language to Stable’s brand identity and improving the brand presence on their website, with a consistent voice tone for their written content and imagery. Later in the project I was also involved with defining task flows and writing user stories in an effort to reform the platform for a 2.0 release.

Stable 2.0

Onboarding experience and platform revamp

Designed an intuitive signup flow that enhanced the onboarding experience for both sales talent and clients, streamlining the process of talent acquisition.

The most exciting part of the project was understanding the current platform and using my UX/UI skills to transform it into the robust 2.0 version we have today.

Conducted an audit of the existing sales-talent management platform and collaborated on documenting detailed user stories to inform the development of Stable 2.0.

Main features and achievement system

Delivered high-fidelity UI designs and interactive elements for key platform features (both client and sales agent facing), including shift calendar management, job applications, contract negotiation and a performance dashboard.

Conceptualized and designed a badge and achievement system to promote positive work etiquette among sales talent, boosting engagement and productivity.
